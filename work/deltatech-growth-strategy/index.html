Project Overview

DeltaTech engaged DigiDhaka for a 6-month strategic advisory program focused on revenue growth in a competitive fintech landscape. We began with a deep audit of their market positioning, customer acquisition funnel, and brand perception.

Our team developed a comprehensive growth strategy — identifying untapped segments, restructuring their digital marketing mix, and introducing a performance measurement framework that gave leadership real-time visibility into growth drivers.

By the end of the engagement, DeltaTech had scaled revenue by 2.4× and established a repeatable playbook for continued growth beyond the advisory period.
